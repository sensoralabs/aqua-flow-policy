# Privacy Policy – Aqua Flow

**Last updated:** 2025

## 1. Introduction
Aqua Flow is a hydration reminder and water tracking mobile application.
We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it.

This app works without any backend server. All user data remains on the user’s device only.

---

## 2. Information We Collect

### Personal Information
We do NOT collect any personally identifiable information such as:
- Name
- Email address
- Phone number
- Account credentials

### Local App Data
The following data is stored locally on your device:
- Water intake history
- Daily hydration goals
- Reminder preferences (2-hour, daily, custom)
- App settings

This data never leaves your device.

---

## 3. Location Information
Aqua Flow may request location permission only if the user allows it.

**Location is used only for:**
- Weather-based hydration recommendations

**Important:**
- Location data is accessed temporarily
- Location data is not stored
- Location data is not shared
- Location tracking does not run in the background

The app functions normally even if location permission is denied.

---

## 4. Notifications & Reminders
The app uses local notifications to:
- Send hydration reminders every 2 hours
- Send daily reminders
- Send custom reminders

All notifications are generated locally on the device.
No server or external service is involved.

---

## 5. Data Export (CSV & PDF)
Users can export hydration data as:
- CSV files
- PDF reports

Exported files:
- Are generated locally on the device
- Are shared only if the user chooses
- Are never accessed or uploaded by us

---

## 6. Local Storage
All app data is stored securely on your device using local storage.
Uninstalling the app permanently deletes all stored data.

---

## 7. Third-Party Services
The app may use public weather APIs to fetch environmental data.

- No personal data is sent
- No analytics SDKs
- No advertising SDKs
- No tracking or profiling

---

## 8. Data Sharing
We do not sell, rent, share, or transfer user data to any third party.

We do not have access to user data.

---

## 9. Children’s Privacy
Aqua Flow does not target children under the age of 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

---

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time.
Any changes will be reflected on this page.
