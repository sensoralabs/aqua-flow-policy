Introduction

Aqua Flow is a hydration reminder and water tracking application. We respect your privacy and do not collect personal data.

Data We Store

  • Water intake history
  • Daily hydration goals
  • Reminder preferences
  • App settings

All data is stored locally on your device.

Location Permission

Location access is optional and used only for weather-based hydration insights. Location data is not stored or shared.

Notifications

Aqua Flow uses local notifications for 2-hour, daily, and custom hydration reminders.
